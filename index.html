Why this exists

You shouldn't need a referral to understand your own body.

Pelvic health physical therapy is one of the most effective, evidence-based specialties in all of rehabilitation medicine. It is also one of the least accessed — because most people don't know it exists, can't find a provider, or have been told their symptoms are simply normal.

Natural Movement Mechanics exists to close that gap. Clinical education, plain language, zero shame.

About Dr. Alli

A Doctor of Physical Therapy.
A farmer. A mother of three. A patient advocate.

Dr. Alli Gannon is a PT, DPT with over 20 years of clinical experience across outpatient orthopedics, pelvic health, acute care, and home health. She spent her first decade-plus practicing in California and Okinawa, Japan before putting down roots in rural Virginia — and has spent two decades watching patients apologize for having a body that needed care.

Natural Movement Mechanics is her answer to that. Clinical education without gatekeeping. Pelvic health without the shame spiral.
